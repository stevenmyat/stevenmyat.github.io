About

Steven Myat is a filmmaker with a passion for capturing the beauty and raw emotions of everyday life. Since 2016, he has been making films and traveling the world with the goal of visiting every country. With a keen eye for detail and emphasis on in-the-moment feelings, Steven brings a visual experience for those who wish to share their very own stories with the world.
